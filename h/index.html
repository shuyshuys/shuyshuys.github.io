The Dewi Cemara Kemang Tourism Park, which was built on an area of 2.6 hectares, certainly adds to the choice of tourism in Kediri, which was officially opened to the public on August 7, 2019.

This park is one of the tourist attractions in Papar, Kediri. This booming tourist spot in Kediri is an artificial place that has several objects that can be enjoyed. In addition, several attractions in this park involve the residents of the Papar District itself.
